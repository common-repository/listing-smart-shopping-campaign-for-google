== Description ==

Also, you can create a new or connect an existing Google Merchant account, or Google Ads account as per your preference. Easily set up and manage your ads budget and campaigns from a centralized WooCommerce dashboard.

 The plugin encompasses a simple and intuitive dashboard that allows you to:
* Create and connect your Google Merchant and Google Ads accounts.
* Automate bulk upload of products from WooCommerce to Google Shopping platform.
* Setup and manage multiple paid ad campaigns for your products. 



==Highlighted features of Listing & Smart Shopping Campaign for Google :==

* **Easy setup and configuration -** Follow the simple steps and configure your Google platform accounts like- Merchant center and Ads accounts to complete the setup of the plugin.

* **Create performance max Campaign -** Just assign a campaign name, select target country, allocate a budget and do a few other advanced settings to finish setting up your performance max campaign

* **Google Ads -** Settings regarding Google Ads, section is further subdivided into 4 sections- 
    </br> a) Accounts - Link/Unlink your Merchant Center Account in just a few clicks. 
    </br> b) Performance Max Campaign - Here you can view and edit your existing performance max campaigns as well as create a new campaign.
    </br> c) Conversions - In this section you can view all your conversions, auto create conversion and upload tag as well as create specific conversion.
    </br> d) Reporting - Download reports of your campaigns in .csv format and analyze the in-depth performance for better decision making.


* **Effortless product profiling -** Create multiple custom profiles for your WooCommerce product categories and map them with the Google categories. This section requires basic configuration details like- target country, content language, currency, product configuration, inventory configuration, and markup configuration. 

* **Reconnect accounts -**This section allows you to change your existing Google Account, Google Merchant Account, and Google Ads account. Also you can view your user id, username and other details configured in the plugin. 

* **Configuration-** This section helps you in configuring your Google Item Id, WooCommerce product and other global configurations which includes your brand, SKU, MPN, and GTIN.

* **Merchant Center -** Merchant center has all the business information including your website information. You also get to claim and verify your website in order to confirm your ownership.

* **Dedicated activities section -** From the activities panel you can view all the recently performed product upload activities with their status as well as the error reasons. Moreover, the activities in progress will also be displayed under the activities tab.

== Installation ==

= Automatic Installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Listing & Smart Shopping Campaign for Google , log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type "Listing & Smart Shopping Campaign for Google" and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our Listing & Smart Shopping Campaign for Google plugin you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our Listing & Smart Shopping Campaign for Google plugin and uploading it to your webserver via your favourite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).
